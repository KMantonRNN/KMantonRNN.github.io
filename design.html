The concepts of Web Design

Important Web Design Practices

Web Design is very different to Web Development; often times there will be 2 teams of people each working on a seperate part. And although they do two very different things, they have to work very closely with one and another to achieve their goals. A Web Designer can only modify existing elements. They cannot create a new part of the website and so must rely on the Web Developer to implement these elements. In this section we will teach you the basic conceptual ideas of Web Design and how to properly apply this to your websites.
